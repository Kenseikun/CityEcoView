Who We Are?

CityEcoView is a photo-comment-based platform allowing users to rank businesses, institutions, and other establishments from the ecological point of view. We are a project that empowers people to make a real change. Our actions evolve around Corporate Social Responsibility and our aim is to provide transparency about how sustainable businesses, institutions and cities really are. Furthermore we promote idea that sustainability is a tool for growing revenue and profitability for business while strengthening their competitive position. Our three goals:

  • Enabling the public participation in the shift towards more sustainable economy
  • Encouraging and supporting establishments in introducing more ecological approach
  • Shifting the mindset that turning ecological can become profitable for the businesses

Mission:
Through transparency and sustainability towards the success for the planet, people and the business.
