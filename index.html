Who We Are?

CityEcoView is a free photo-feedback-based platform allowing users to upload their eco observations, both positive and negative. These observations then become available for the rest of the community including businesses and local authorities.

We strongly believe that constructive but innovative actions have to be taken given the current state of our environment.

Our aim is to unite people in their efforts toward sustainability and to raise awareness and eco transparency among people and communities.

    Our three goals:

  • Enabling the public participation in the shift towards more sustainable economy

  • Encouraging and supporting establishments in introducing more ecological approach

  • Shifting the mindset that turning ecological can become profitable for the businesses


Mission:
Inspiring our community to take easy and innovative actions for more sustainable world

Functionality

User can find the business on the map and see it’s overall rating. After clicking the average score will break down to all different categories, for which businesses has been rated. In case business is not showing on our database user can simply add it.

We are based in Warsaw. The pilot run of our MVP will also take place in here. However, we are not limiting our data collection only to Warsaw. We have number of dedicated volueters who provide us with regular data input from London, Tel Aviv and New York. We keep growing on unprecedented scale.

Why Warsaw?

Warsaw is a great place to find very skillful staff that are committed to what they do. It is a good place for young business as well as local and foreign investors thanks to non-equity programs.

Moreover, Warsaw is a young and vibrant city which leads trends in Poland. Recently, Warsaw has been named as the sixth most vegan-friendly city in the world by the leading international guide for vegan restaurants. The top three cities in Happy Cow’s ranking are London, New York and Berlin.

This shows that Warsaw is modern and tries to be ecological at least from the restaurants’ point of view.
